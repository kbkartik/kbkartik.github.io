Research

My main research interests lie at learning robust representations, safe policies, and hierarchical skills for assistive tasks. Following are some questions which motivate my research interests:

  1. How can we learn robust representations so that agents can safely perceive and act in the world?
  2. How to learn skill policies and their representations for effective re-usability and generalization?
  3. How can we provide safety guarantees for the skills learnt?
